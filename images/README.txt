VCSAR Team 1 — Photos
=====================

Drop your photos in THIS folder using the exact filenames below.
Until a file exists, the site shows a styled placeholder in its place,
so you can add them one at a time. JPG is preferred; .jpg extension required.

  hero.jpg      Home hero — big wide action shot. ~1920 x 1080 (landscape).
                Pick your most dramatic image: rope rescue, ridgeline, golden hour.

  mission.jpg   Home "worst day" section — vertical shot. ~1000 x 1250 (4:5 portrait).

  team.jpg      Home recruit band — group/team or training scene. ~1920 x 900 (wide).

  training.jpg  Join page top banner — training day. ~1920 x 900 (wide).

  recruits.jpg  Join page "what to expect" — recruits in action. ~1600 x 1000 (16:10).

  gear.jpg      Donate page top banner — gear / team at work. ~1920 x 900 (wide).

Tips
----
- Landscape banners look best wide and a bit tall; faces/action toward the
  center-left won't get cropped by text overlays.
- Large files are fine, but for fast loading aim for under ~500 KB each
  (you can compress at squoosh.app or tinyjpg.com).
- Want different filenames? Just tell me and I'll re-point the site.
