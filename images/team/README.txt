VCSAR Team 1 — Member Headshots
===============================

Put each member's photo in THIS folder, then list the member in the
`members` array near the bottom of team.html.

Photo tips
----------
- Portrait orientation works best (the cards crop to a 4:5 frame).
- ~800 x 1000 px is plenty. Keep each file under ~300 KB.
- Name the files simply, e.g.  jsmith.jpg, mgarcia.jpg  — then reference
  that exact path in team.html, e.g.  photo: "images/team/jsmith.jpg"

For each member you can set:
  name   — full name
  role   — position / specialty (Team Captain, Medical Lead, Rope Tech, etc.)
  since  — the year they joined (cards auto-sort by seniority and show "X yrs")
  photo  — images/team/<file>.jpg
  quote  — optional one-line "what SAR means to me"

Until a photo is added, the card shows a clean silhouette placeholder,
so you can fill the roster in gradually. Send me the names/roles/years and
photos and I'll populate it all for you.
